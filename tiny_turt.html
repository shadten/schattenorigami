Tiny Turt

Version 1.0

This is a relatively simple model that does not employ any special design technique and requires almost no shaping after the actual folding process. It can be folded very quickly too, it takes me about 15 minutes. Yet when folded carefully, it has a very nice effect.

    Created September 2021
    Paper 15cm square of tracing paper + tissue paper
    Time ~15 minutes
