Cetratus rubropunctatus

box-pleated spider

Version 1.0

I saw a picture of a Cetratus species here on Instagram and immediately thought that it would be a great subject to try and design an origami model of it. So here is what I came up with. I learned a lot while designing this one and it turned out quite challenging. There are some things I'd like to change in future revisions but I like how it turned out.

    Created January 2020
    Paper 41 cm square of double tissue
    Time ~8 hours
    Grid 64x64
    Note Additional details for the eyes and body shown only on the right half of the CP.
