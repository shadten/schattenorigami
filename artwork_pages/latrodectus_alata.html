Latrodectus alata

box-pleated color change spider

Version 1.0

I have once again emerged from the depths of exams, assignments and lack of motivation to present to you this little model I designed some months ago and then forgot to post. I am currently pretending that posting this on Halloween was my plan all along.
Anyway, the model is a hybrid of two other of my spider designs, the Redback Spider (Latrodectus hasselti) and the winged Tarantula (Avicularia alata). To be honest, I am not completely satisfied with how the shaping turned out - especially the legs - but on the other hand it's good enough to not make me want to refold it any time soon.

    Created March 2022
    Paper 47 cm square of double tissue
    Time ~12 hours
    Grid 52x52
    Note Details to create the spikes on the legs are only shown on the right half of the CP.
