Loch Ness Monster

box-pleated legendary creature

Version 2.0

This is a complete redisign of my original model, the structure is completely different compared to the previous version (which had diagonal symmetry and 22.5° structures, while the new one has book symmetry and is mainly pure boxpleating). The body is based on a 24x24 grid, the neck and head require a 96x96 grid.
Shaping took longer than expected and I put a coin in the back of the body to balance out the heavy head/neck part
It turned out pretty much like i imagined it, though I think theres still room for improvements.

    Created October 2019
    Paper 49 cm square of double tissue
    Time ~6.5 hours
    Grid 24x24 with partial 96
