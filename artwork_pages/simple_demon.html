Simple Demon

box-pleated color change humanoid

Version 1.0

I came up with this design when I was playing around with a test fold of a CP that I drew almost 2 years ago (i.e. in 2018). I wanted to design a simple humanoid model for quite a while now, so unexpectedly stumbling across this structure was very pleasing. It can be modified pretty easily, which makes it interesting to play around with.
I didn't include the colour change of the tail in the CP because I thought It'd be more confusing than helpful, but it can be done just by bringing the flap to the back between the legs and then wrapping the layers around.

    Created September 2020
    Paper 20 cm square of double tissue
    Time ~2 hours
    Grid 16x16 parallel to edges (= 32x32 diagonal)
