Redback Spider

Version 1.0

I had the idea to design a spider with a colour change on the abdomen a few weeks ago and first tried to design it using box pleating (like I usually do). This time, though, I ended up using Robert Lang's TreeMaker, so it's a circle packed structure. (Actually my first real non-box pleated design.) Although there are some things I'd like to change in future revisions, I'm really happy with how it turned out.
The reference point on the bottom can be measured, about 0.4058 from the left edge. Or you can use the folding sequence indicated by the light blue lines to find another reference point. The diagonal through the bottom right corner brings the bottom left corner of the paper to the vertical central line. Thanks to bodo_ori for showing me this folding sequence!
